## Your first visit, made simple
We keep new patient visits calm, clear, and efficient. Expect a friendly welcome, digital imaging, and a dentist-led exam with options and costs explained before we begin. Serving families and professionals across Sampletown.

### What to expect
- Digital X-rays and a gentle, thorough exam
- Photos or scans to show what we see
- Clear options, timelines, and costs

### Comfort-first visit
- Comfort menu, blankets, and headphones
- Gentle anesthesia when needed
- Calm, step-by-step communication

### After your visit
- Same-day estimates and financing guidance
- Follow-up texts you can reply to
- Clear next steps to keep you on track

### What to bring
- Photo ID and insurance card (if applicable)
- List of medications or health conditions
- Questions or smile goals you want to discuss
- Links to forms or membership portal

### Insurance & payment
We accept most major PPO plans and verify benefits for you. Expect transparent estimates before treatment starts. Membership options are available for families without insurance, along with financing guidance for larger treatments.

### FAQs
**Do you take my insurance?**
We work with most major PPO plans and will verify benefits before treatment. Share your plan details when scheduling so we can provide clear estimates.

**What if I’m nervous about dental visits?**
We’re comfort-focused. Expect a calm pace, headphones, blankets, and gentle anesthesia when appropriate. Tell us your concerns—we’ll tailor the visit.

**How long is the first appointment?**
Plan about 60–90 minutes for a comprehensive exam, digital X-rays, photos, and a consultation with the doctor.

**Do you offer financing?**
Yes. We provide transparent estimates, help you maximize benefits, and offer financing guidance for larger treatments.
