## Clear, flexible options for your care
We verify benefits, share transparent estimates, and offer flexible payment options so you can plan confidently—whether you need preventive visits, cosmetic dentistry, or dental implants in Sampletown.

### Insurance
We work with most major PPO plans. Share your insurance info when scheduling and we’ll verify coverage before your visit.

### Transparent estimates
Expect clear estimates, options, and timelines before treatment starts so you can decide with confidence.

### Guidance
Ask us about benefits, EOBs, and payment timing—we’ll help simplify the process.

### Payment options
- All major credit and debit cards
- HSA/FSA cards
- Financing available for larger treatments
- Phased care plans to fit your budget

### Membership plan
No insurance? Our in-house membership includes preventive care plus member savings on additional treatment—no deductibles, waiting periods, or yearly maximums.

### FAQs
**Do you take my insurance?**
We work with most major PPO plans. Share your information when booking and we’ll verify benefits before your visit.

**Can I see costs before treatment?**
Yes. We provide clear estimates and explain timelines, options, and any out-of-pocket costs before you decide.

**Do you offer payment plans?**
We offer financing guidance for larger treatments and can phase care to fit your budget.

**What if I don’t have insurance?**
Our membership plan includes preventive care and member discounts on additional treatment—no deductibles or waiting periods.
